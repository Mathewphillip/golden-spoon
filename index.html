from land

Since we began in 2002, we have made it our mission to serve only the very best steak. We work tirelessly with local farmers to source some of the best of British produce from the finest local producers, ensuring we remain focused on sustainability and traceable origins. Each cut is expertly butchered and aged and cooked on the Southbend grill to lock in the flavour and create an irresistible caramelised crust.
